Bee Facts

Fact #1

Bee populations play a crucial role in pollinating many of the world's crops, contributing to global food production.

Fact #2

Bees communicate with each other by dancing. The waggle dance indicates the direction and distance to a food source.

Fact #3

Bees are known for producing honey, but they also play a role in producing beeswax, royal jelly, and pollen.

Fact #4

Bees have five eyes - two large compound eyes and three smaller simple eyes. They can see ultraviolet light, which helps them find nectar.

Fact #5

Bees are capable of flying at speeds of up to 15 miles per hour and can beat their wings about 200 times per second.

Fact #6

Bees are critical to the biodiversity of our planet. They pollinate flowers, which helps plants reproduce and grow.
